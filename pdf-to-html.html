Frequently Asked Questions

                

                    

                        

                            
                        

                        

                            
Can I convert PDF to HTML for free?

                            

                                Yes! Our PDF to HTML converter is completely free with no limitations on usage.
                            

                        

                    

                    
                    

                        

                            
                        

                        

                            
Is my PDF data secure when using this tool?

                            

                                Yes! All PDF processing happens directly in your browser. Your files never leave your computer, ensuring complete privacy.
                            

                        

                    

                    
                    

                        

                            
                        

                        

                            
Can I preview the HTML before downloading?

                            

                                Absolutely!

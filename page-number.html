Frequently Asked Questions

Can I customize the position and style of page numbers?

Yes! You can choose from multiple positions, customize font, size, color, and choose from various numbering formats.

Is my PDF data secure when using this tool?

Yes! All PDF processing happens directly in your browser. Your files never leave your computer, ensuring complete privacy.

Can I add page numbers to specific pages only?

Absolutely! You can choose to add page numbers to all pages or specify a custom page range.

What types of numbering formats are available?

We offer multiple formats including simple numbers, Roman numerals, letters, and custom formats with total page count.

Is this PDF numbering tool completely free?

Yes, our PDF page numbering tool is completely free with no limitations on usage.

Will the numbered PDF maintain original quality?

Yes, all page numbers are added as vector elements, preserving the original quality of your PDF document.

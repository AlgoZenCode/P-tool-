Frequently Asked Questions

What types of PDF files can I add page numbers to?

Our PDF page numbering tool works with all standard PDF files, including those with text, images, forms, and scanned documents.

Is my data secure when adding page numbers to PDF?

Yes! All PDF processing happens directly in your browser. Your files never leave your computer, ensuring complete privacy.

Can I customize the position and style of page numbers?

Absolutely! You can choose from multiple positions, customize font, size, color, and choose from various numbering formats.

Can I add page numbers to specific pages only?

Yes, you can choose to add page numbers to all pages or specify a custom page range for precise control.

Is this PDF page numbering tool completely free?

Yes, our PDF page numbering tool is completely free with no limitations on the number of pages you can process.

Will the original PDF quality be preserved?

Yes, all page numbers are added as vector elements, preserving the original quality of your PDF document.

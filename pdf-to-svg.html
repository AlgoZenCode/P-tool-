Frequently Asked Questions

What is SVG format and why use it?

SVG (Scalable Vector Graphics) is a vector image format that can be scaled to any size without losing quality. It's perfect for logos, icons, and illustrations.

Is my PDF data secure when using this tool?

Yes! All PDF processing happens directly in your browser. Your files never leave your computer, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Can I convert specific pages instead of the whole PDF?

Absolutely! You can convert all pages, a specific page range, or individual pages from your PDF document.

What are the advantages of SVG over other formats?

SVG files are scalable, have smaller file sizes for simple graphics, can be edited with code, and are perfect for web use with excellent browser support.

Is this PDF to SVG converter completely free?

Yes, our PDF to SVG converter is completely free with no limitations on usage or watermarks on your output files.

Can I download SVGs individually or as a zip?

Yes, you can download each SVG separately or choose to download all converted SVG files at once in a ZIP archive.

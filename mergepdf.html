Frequently Asked Questions

How many PDF files can I merge at once?

You can merge unlimited PDF files in one go. Our tool handles documents of all sizes efficiently.

Yes! All PDF processing happens directly in your browser. Your files never leave your computer, ensuring complete privacy.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is PDF metadata and why should I remove it?

PDF metadata contains hidden information like author name, creation date, software used, and other sensitive details that can compromise your privacy. Removing it helps protect your information.

Is my PDF data secure when using this tool?

Yes! All PDF processing happens directly in your browser. Your files never leave your computer, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What types of metadata can be removed?

You can remove author name, creation date, modification date, document title, subject, keywords, creator software, producer software, and other hidden metadata.

Will removing metadata affect my PDF content?

No, removing metadata only removes the hidden information. Your actual PDF content, text, images, and formatting remain completely unchanged.

Is this metadata removal tool completely free?

Yes, our PDF metadata removal tool is completely free with no limitations on usage or file size.

Can I see what metadata exists before removing it?

Yes, our tool shows you all existing metadata before removal, so you know exactly what information will be cleaned from your document.

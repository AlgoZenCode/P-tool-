Frequently Asked Questions

What is PDF metadata and why should I remove it?

PDF metadata contains information like author name, creation date, modification date, software used, and other sensitive details that can compromise your privacy.

Is my data secure when removing PDF metadata?

Yes! All PDF processing happens directly in your browser. Your files never leave your computer, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Can I choose which metadata to remove?

Absolutely! You can choose to remove all metadata or selectively remove specific information like author name, creation date, or software information.

Will removing metadata affect my PDF content?

No, removing metadata only removes the hidden information about the document. Your actual content, text, images, and formatting remain completely unchanged.

Is this metadata remover completely free?

Yes, our PDF metadata remover is completely free with no limitations on the number of files you can process.

What types of PDF files can I process?

Our tool works with all standard PDF files, including those with text, images, forms, and scanned documents.

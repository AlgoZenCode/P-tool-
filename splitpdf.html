Frequently Asked Questions

How do I select pages from my PDF?

After uploading your PDF, you'll see thumbnails of all pages. Simply click on the pages you want to select, or use the 'Select All' option to choose all pages at once.

Is my PDF data secure when using this tool?

Yes! All PDF processing happens directly in your browser. Your files never leave your computer, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Can I download selected pages as separate PDFs?

Yes! You can download each selected page as an individual PDF file, or download all selected pages combined into a single PDF document.

What if I want to select a range of pages?

You can easily select a range of pages by clicking the first page, holding Shift, and clicking the last page in the range. All pages between will be selected automatically.

Is this PDF page selector completely free?

Yes, our PDF page selector is completely free with no limitations on usage or the number of pages you can extract.

What happens if I select no pages?

The extract button will remain disabled until you select at least one page from your PDF document.
